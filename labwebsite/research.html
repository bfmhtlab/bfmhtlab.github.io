Bio-Fluid Mechanics

We congregate clinicians and engineers to improve the decision-making ability of life-threatening problems in the human circulatory system. Taking Bio medical images/signals as input, we perform the patient-specific CFD/FSI simulations backed with ML/AI techniques to understand and predict the underlying risks.

Heat Transfer

We focus on theoretical and computational research in the field of heat and mass transfer with an emphasis on contributions that will increase the basic understanding of the processes and their application to engineering problems. Our research efforts are mostly on but not limited to the areas of nuclear power plant cooling techniques, energy engineering and environmental applications of heat and/or mass transfer
